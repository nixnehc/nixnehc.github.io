Maria Manzano (1999), Model Theory, Oxford Logic Guides: 37. (English edition, original Spanish 1990)

才知道 Maria Manzano 是位女逻辑学家。

这本书不包括 Stability theory (Morley) 方面的内容。

  • Model theory: mathematical structures <~~~> formal languages

  • Model theory become as a discipline until the 1950s. The name “model theory” was used for the first time by Tarski in 1954.

  • the Compactness theorem is a simple corollary from the Completeness thm., but without appealing to the notion of deducibility, we can obtain a purely semantic proof: by combining the models of finite sets of formulas to construct that of the infinite one.
    ultrafilter + ultraproduct ==> a model of the infinite set of formulas.

    • another way of proving compactness, via. the concept of a diagram.
